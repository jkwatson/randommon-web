Unnatural Selection

The Wandering Monstrum includes monsters from Unnatural Selection, a bestiary supplement for Shadowdark RPG created by the Dungeon Damsel. Unnatural Selection features a menagerie of wonderfully weird and dangerous creatures designed to surprise and delight players and game masters alike.

If you enjoy the monsters you find here, we encourage you to visit dungeondamsel.com and support the Dungeon Damsel's work directly.
