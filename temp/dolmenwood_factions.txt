The Major Factions and Powers
Atanuwë (The Nag-Lord): A chaotic interloper who rules from the Sargstone, seeking to transform Dolmenwood into a playground of horrific absurdity and police-state terror
.
The Cold Prince: The exiled liege of Frigia who seeks to destroy the Ring of Chell and restore an eternal fairy winter
.
The Drune: A secretive cabal of pale, raven-haired sorcerers who guard the standing stones and the Ring of Chell. They are the last faction that still remembers the truth of the ancient Triple Compact
.
Human Nobility: Led by Duke Thespian III, these noble houses (such as Harrowmoor and Guillefer) rule the settled regions. They seek to maintain order and are increasingly hostile toward the Drune
.
Longhorn Nobility: Aristocratic breggles who rule the High Wold. They are ancient, proud, and currently embroiled in bitter rivalries between their primary houses: Ramius, Murkin, and Malbleat
.
Pluritine Church: The dominant monotheistic faith, headquartered in Castle Brackenwold. They aim to purge the Wood of "devil-worshipping" Drunes and Witches while searching for their own lost wayside shrines
.
Witches: Secretive covens who worship the Gwyrigons (Wood Gods). They maintain ancient sites and wait for the "Hoaghryn," a prophesied child ruler of all witches
.
Ygraine Mordlin: A mysterious, ancient sorceress who rules the village of Meagre's Reach, which she "dredged" out of a bubble of history using dimension-twisting magic
